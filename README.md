# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales data using **SQL (MySQL)** and **Power BI** to uncover business insights related to sales performance, profitability, product performance, customer behavior, and regional trends.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* 🗄️ MySQL
* 💻 SQL
* 📈 Power BI
* 📄 CSV Dataset
* 📊 Data Visualization

## 💡 Key Business Insights

### 💰 Revenue Performance

* The business generated **$2.27M** in total sales revenue.

### 📈 Profitability

* The company earned **$282.86K** in profit.
* Overall profit margin was **12.45%**.

### 🏆 Product Performance

* Technology products generated the highest sales revenue.
* Top-performing products contributed significantly to overall revenue.

### 🌎 Regional Performance

* The West region contributed the largest share of sales revenue.

### 👥 Customer Insights

* A small group of customers generated a significant portion of total sales.
